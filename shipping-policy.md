# Shipping Policy

*At **AromaxCreation**, we strive to ensure your products reach you safely and promptly.*

---

## Domestic Shipping (India)

* **Carrier:** Orders are shipped through registered domestic courier companies.
* **Dispatch Time:** Dispatch is done within **0–7 business days** from the date of order confirmation and payment, or as per the delivery date agreed upon at the time of purchase.
* **Delivery Time:** Delivery timelines depend on the courier company/postal authorities and your delivery location.
* **Liability:** We are not liable for delays caused by courier companies, postal services, or factors beyond our control. Our commitment is to hand over your order to the courier/postal service within the stipulated dispatch period.

## Delivery Address

* All orders will be delivered to the shipping address provided at the time of purchase.
* Please ensure that the **address and contact details are accurate** to avoid delivery delays or failed deliveries.

## Order Confirmation & Tracking

* **Confirmation:** Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation message/email at the registered contact details provided during checkout.
* **Tracking:** Tracking details will be shared so you can easily monitor your shipment’s progress.
