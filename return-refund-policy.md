# Return & Refund Policy

At **Aromax Creation**, we are committed to delivering an exceptional luxury fragrance experience. Please review our policies carefully before placing your order to ensure complete clarity and transparency.

---

## 1. General Return Policy
**No Returns on Opened or Used Products**  
Due to hygiene and safety reasons, we **do not accept returns or exchanges** once a product has been delivered, opened, or used.

### Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Products
If you receive a damaged, defective, leaked, or incorrectly shipped product, please contact our Customer Care team within **24 hours** of delivery.

---

## 2. Eligibility for Refund or Replacement
Refunds or replacements are applicable **only** in the following cases:
*   Damaged product
*   Manufacturing defect
*   Incorrect item received

### Mandatory Requirements for Claims:
To process your request, you must provide:
1.  **An unboxing video** clearly showing the product in its original sealed packaging.
2.  **Clear images** of the damaged or incorrect item.

### Reporting Deadlines:
*   **Missing or Incorrect Items:** Must be reported within **24 hours** of delivery with valid proof.
*   **Transit Damage/Leakage:** Must be reported within **24–36 hours** of delivery.

---

## 3. Important Considerations

### Product Sensitivities
Our perfumes are crafted using premium aromatic compounds and natural extracts. Individual skin types may react differently. 
*   We are **not responsible** for allergic reactions or sensitivities. 
*   We strongly recommend performing a **patch test** before full application.

### Non-Delivery
If your order is marked as **“Delivered”** but you have not received it, please notify us within **48 hours**. Claims made after this period will not be eligible for review.

---

## 4. Refund Processing
*   **Approval Time:** Approved refunds will be processed within **5–7 business days**.
*   **Payment Method:** Refunds will be credited only to the original payment method used at checkout.
*   **Delayed Refunds:** If more than **15 business days** have passed after refund approval and you have not received the amount, please contact us.

---

## 5. Non-Returnable Items
We do not accept returns for:
*   Opened or used products
*   Custom or personalized items
*   Items purchased during special promotions or sale events
*   Gift cards

---

## 6. Cancellation Policy

### Before Dispatch
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. A **2% payment gateway fee** will be deducted from the refund amount for all prepaid orders.

### After Dispatch
*   Once an order has been dispatched, **cancellations cannot be processed**.
*   If a prepaid order is returned to us due to refusal or failed delivery, a **₹75 handling fee** will be deducted from the refund.

### Refunds for Cancelled Orders
Approved cancellations will be refunded within **4–5 business days** to the original payment method.

---

## 7. Delivery & Payment

### Damaged Package on Delivery
If your package appears tampered with or physically damaged at the time of delivery, please **refuse to accept it** and inform us immediately.

### Payment Policy
*   Currently, Aromax Creation accepts **prepaid orders only**.
*   **Cash on Delivery (COD)** is not available at this time.
